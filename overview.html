Snow Leopard

Mission Statement

To build a smart, searchable platform that enhances the way Cognizant Team Leads match consultants to projects by leveraging structured profile data from SharePoint. Our goal is to streamline staffing decisions, reduce manual effort, and improve project alignment through efficient search, filtering, and profile insights.

Problem Statement

Cognizant is an IT consulting company that staffs consultants to IT-related projects. Team Leads often rely on word of mouth or digging through SharePoint folders to find consultants with the right skills for a project. The goal is to make staffing decisions faster and more accurate by surfacing the most relevant consultants for each project.
